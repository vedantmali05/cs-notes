Communication Media

Physical means of sending, delivering and receiving verbal or non-verbal information or data is the Communication or Transmission Media

Anything that can send or receive information from source to destinition is the transmission medium.

The transmission medium is usually a free space, metallic cable, fiber optic cable, etc. Based on the way a data is being transmitted, means of data communication is divided into two categories:- Guided or Wired Media like Twisted Pair Cable, Coaxial Cable and Optical Fibres. Unguided or Unbounded or Wireless Media like microwaves, radiowaves or infrared.
